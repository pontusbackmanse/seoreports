SEO Research Report: AB Werkt


Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive SEO analysis for AB Werkt, a recruitment and staffing agency operating in Southern Netherlands. The analysis focuses on their current organic search performance, keyword opportunities, competitive landscape, and backlink profile in the Dutch market, with actionable recommendations to improve their search visibility and outrank competitors.


Company Overview

AB Werkt is a recruitment and staffing agency connecting job seekers with employers primarily in Southern Netherlands. They specialize in sectors including agriculture (both animal and plant), industry, waste management, landscaping, and cleaning. The company employs over 6,000 people annually and processes more than 100,000 salary slips each year, indicating their significant market presence. (Company Website)
