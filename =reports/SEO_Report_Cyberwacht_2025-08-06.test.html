Cyberwacht operates as a telephone helpline service that provides immediate assistance to individuals and businesses who have been hacked or suspect they have been hacked. Their core services include:

  • Immediate assistance via telephone helpline
  • Malware removal from infected devices
  • Investigation of cyberattack causes
  • Damage limitation following hacks
  • General computer problem solving

Their pricing model offers the first 5 minutes free, followed by €1.49 per minute up to a maximum of €49 per consultation. They operate 6 days a week and have established partnerships with organizations like Student aan Huis. Company Website
