SEO Research Report: Het Rooster


Company Overview

Het Rooster is a Dutch company that provides self-rostering software designed to help organizations manage staff scheduling efficiently. Their platform emphasizes giving employees freedom and responsibility in managing their own schedules. Key features include user-friendliness, time-saving capabilities, a mobile app, and ISO 27001 certification for security. (Company Website)


The software offers various functionalities including:

  • Self-rostering and inroostering
  • Creating fixed and flexible schedules
  • Managing employee profiles
  • Hours registration and absence management
  • Communication tools (staff directory and message board)
  • Reporting capabilities

Their target market includes various organizations such as museums, healthcare facilities, libraries, and sports clubs. (Company Website)
