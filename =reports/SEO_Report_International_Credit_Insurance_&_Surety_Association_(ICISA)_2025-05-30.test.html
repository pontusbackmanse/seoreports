Company Overview

The International Credit Insurance & Surety Association (ICISA) brings together the world's leading providers of trade credit insurance and surety bonds. Founded in 1928, ICISA advocates on behalf of its members on issues affecting credit insurance and surety (Company Website). The organization facilitates discussions on major industry developments through committees and working groups, covering areas such as insurance regulation, banking rules, economic impact, technology & innovation, and environmental and social governance (Company Website).


ICISA is headquartered in Amsterdam, Netherlands, with its head office located at Herengracht 473, Amsterdam 1017 BS, and an EU office at Spaces Schiphol Airport, Evert van de Beekstraat 354, Amsterdam 1118 CZ (Lobby Facts).
