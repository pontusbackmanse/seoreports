SEO Research Report: Kaartje2go


Company Overview

Kaartje2go is an online platform based in the Netherlands that specializes in personalized greeting cards and gifts. Their core business allows users to create and send customized cards for various occasions including birthdays, weddings, birth announcements, and holidays. The platform features an online card maker where users can add personal photos, text, and design elements. Additionally, they offer complementary gifts such as chocolates, flowers, and balloons that can be sent alongside cards. Customers can choose to have cards sent directly to recipients or delivered to themselves for personal handover. (Company Website)


Clarification:
  • Emphasize gift add-ons (flowers, balloons) in SEO landing pages to differentiate from pure card competitors.
