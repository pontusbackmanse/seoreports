1. Company Overview

MuzikantenWIKI (https://muzikantenwiki.nl) is a Dutch content platform serving as a comprehensive resource for musicians in the Netherlands. The website offers various content types including:

  • Detailed chord charts, tabs, and lyrics for popular songs
  • Guitar tutorials and guides for beginners and advanced players
  • Product comparisons and buying guides for musical equipment
  • Reviews of music gear and products
  • News related to the music industry

The platform primarily targets Dutch-speaking musicians looking for practical resources to support their musical activities, with a particular focus on guitar players.
