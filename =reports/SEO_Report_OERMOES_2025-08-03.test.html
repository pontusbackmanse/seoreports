The analysis reveals a concerning 47.60% decrease in organic visibility over the past year, with significant drops in top-ranking positions. Despite this decline, several high-performing pages related to specific plant guides and recipes show strong potential. Our keyword analysis identifies valuable opportunities in vegetable harvesting and growing content, particularly for terms like "spinazie oogsten" and "andijvie kweken" which have favorable search volumes and low competition. Competitive analysis reveals that makkelijkemoestuin.nl and groei.nl are outperforming OERMOES.nl on high-value keywords, with effective content strategies focused on simplifying gardening concepts and providing seasonal guidance. Technical analysis indicates hosting with MyFastHosting and several critical technical SEO gaps including missing analytics tools and potential performance issues. This report outlines actionable recommendations to reverse the negative trend and capitalize on untapped opportunities in the Dutch gardening and cooking content market.
