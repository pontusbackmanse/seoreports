SEO Research Report: TafelsmaaK


Company Overview

TafelsmaaK is a family-owned business specializing in custom-made furniture including tables, cabinets, and other pieces according to customer specifications. They also offer steelwork services. The company operates a showroom and workshop in Groningen, Netherlands, and has produced over 5000 custom furniture pieces to date. They have an impressive customer satisfaction rating of 9.8 out of 10, and emphasize high quality, custom designs, and social responsibility in their business approach. (Company Website)
