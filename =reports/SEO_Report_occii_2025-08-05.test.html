Company Overview

OCCII (Onafhankelijk Cultureel Centrum) is an independent cultural center and alternative music venue located in Amsterdam. Established in a formerly squatted building that was legalized in 1992, OCCII hosts a diverse range of music genres including global beats, noise, punk, folk, improv, rave, avant-garde, and electronic music. The organization operates as a non-profit, creating a space for subcultures and non-commercial collaborations, and also runs "Kinderpret," a program of theater performances and workshops for children. OCCII is part of De Binnenpret, a social center comprising various cultural and political collectives. Source: [Company Website]
